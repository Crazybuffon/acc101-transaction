🎓 Accounting 101 Game: Mastering Transactions

👩‍🏫 Developed by Professor Grace Haoqing Fan, Singapore Management University

Welcome, Accounting 101 students! Ready to make learning accounting fun and interactive? Here's how to play:

  • Start with a Transaction: Each round presents 10 real-world business scenarios for you to tackle.
  • Journal Entries: Enter the correct debits and credits to record the transaction.
  • T-Accounts: Visualize your individual accounts by updating the T-Accounts.
  • Trial Balance: Ensure your books are balanced by updating the Trial Balance.
  • Check Your Answers: Validate your entries and see if you're on the right track.
  • Advance to the Next Transaction: Once you've mastered the current one, move forward to the next challenge.

After completing all 10 transactions, you can refresh the page to receive a new set of transactions if you’d like to continue practicing. Follow each step, learn from your mistakes, and watch your accounting skills grow. Let’s get started and turn those numbers into knowledge!

📚 Key Accounting Rules:

• Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity

• Debits must equal Credits

• Debit: Increases Assets & Expenses, Decreases Liabilities, Revenue, & Equity

• Credit: Decreases Assets & Expenses, Increases Liabilities, Revenue, & Equity
